CTRL

SZA's debut studio album "Ctrl," released in 2017, marked a significant moment in contemporary R&B music. With its blend of soulful vocals, introspective lyrics, and eclectic production, "Ctrl" received widespread critical acclaim and commercial success. The album's exploration of themes like love, self-discovery, and empowerment resonated deeply with audiences, establishing SZA as a major figure in the music industry.

SNOOZE

"Snooze" is a standout track from SZA's second studio album "SOS," released in 2022. This dreamy ballad reflects on the complexities of relationships and themes of self-worth, featuring poignant lyrics and captivating melodies. "Snooze" garnered widespread acclaim, topping charts and earning accolades such as Best R&B Song at the Grammy Awards. Its success further solidified SZA's position as a leading voice in contemporary R&B music.
